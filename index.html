WHO WE ARE?

Gyanshala: Empowering Rural Education

Started in 2022, Gyanshala is more than just a place of study—it’s a hub for holistic growth. Committed to providing quality education in rural areas, it focuses on overall student development, including academics, sports, digital literacy, and public speaking. Under the personal guidance of Mr. Iliyash, Gyanshala ensures individualized support, empowering students to excel and contribute to society meaningfully.
